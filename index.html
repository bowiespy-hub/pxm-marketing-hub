Team Structure (14 Members)團隊結構（14人）

Content Team Leader
內容組長
1 position
Oversees all content output. Plans monthly content calendar, manages Content Pillar balance (25/25/20/30), quality control on all posts before publishing, coaches and guides the 3 Content Producers. 統籌所有內容產出。規劃月度內容日曆、管理四大支柱比例、發佈前品質把關、指導3位 Content Producer。
Monthly Planning月度規劃→ Assign Tasks分配任務→ Quality Review品質審核→ Performance Tracking成效追蹤
Content Producer
內容製作人
3 positions
Full-cycle content execution. From ideation, scripting, shooting (including YouTube shooting & planning), editing short-form videos (Reels / Stories / Clips), writing captions, to scheduling publication. Complex or large-scale videos are escalated to Video Editor. 全流程內容執行。從發想、腳本、拍攝（含 YouTube 拍攝與規劃）、剪輯短片（Reels / Stories / Clips）、撰寫文案、到排程發佈。複雜或大型影片移交 Video Editor 處理。
Ideation→ Script→ Shoot→ Edit→ Caption→ Publish
Branding Executive
品牌執行
2 positions
Brand strategy and development. Creates recruitment materials, manages ad campaigns, maintains website, produces Recruitment Kit & Company Profile, handles external partnerships and media collaborations. 品牌策略與發展。製作招募素材、管理廣告投放、維護網站、製作 Recruitment Kit 和 Company Profile、處理對外合作與媒體聯繫。
Brand Strategy→ Recruitment Materials→ Ad Management→ Website & Digital
Designer
設計師
3 positions
All visual design output. Achiever posters (250+/month), Open House designs (20-30/month), training posters (10-15/month), festive greetings, event key visuals, stage & backdrop design, print collateral. 所有平面視覺產出。成就海報（250+/月）、開放日設計（20-30/月）、培訓海報（10-15/月）、節慶祝賀、活動主視覺 Key Visual、舞台背景設計、印刷物料。
Receive Brief→ Design→ Review→ Revision→ Deliver
Video Editor
影片剪輯師
3 positions
Primary focus: YouTube long-form editing (2-3 episodes/month for Brew Talk, Vibe Sync, PropTalks series). Additionally: 1-2 high-quality productions per month, major event films. Includes VFX, color grading, subtitles, and thumbnail creation. 主力：YouTube 長片剪輯（每月2-3集，Brew Talk、Vibe Sync、PropTalks 系列）。加開：每月1-2支高品質影片、大型活動紀錄影片。含特效、調色、字幕、Thumbnail 製作。
Receive Footage→ Rough Cut→ VFX / Color→ Subtitle→ Thumbnail→ Upload
Copywriter / SOP Manager
文案 / SOP 管理
1 position
All written content: blog articles, subtitle proofing, training & event copywriting. Also responsible for SOP creation & maintenance, and keeping this Knowledge Hub up to date. 所有文字內容：Blog 撰寫、字幕校對、培訓與活動文案。同時負責 SOP 建立與維護、Knowledge Hub 內容更新。
Research→ Write→ Proofread→ Publish / Archive
Admin Coordinator
行政協調
1 position
Cross-department coordination, data collection & organization, scheduling management, Google Drive filing & organization, internal administration, meeting minutes & follow-up tracking. 跨部門協調、資料收集與整理、排程管理、Google Drive 歸檔與整理、內部行政事務、會議記錄與跟進追蹤。
